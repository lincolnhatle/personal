Cybot Challenge

Final Project for CPRE 288 Embedded Systems. Language C. Programmed cybots equipped with ARM Cortex-4 Processors and to simulate a "mars rover" mission where we blind navigated an obstacle course to the finish line. Made use of IR, cliff, and sound sensors. We used a GUI built in Rust to map out the course and cybot's position on the screen while we maneuvered it.

Ray Tracer Cornell Box

Final project for COSC363 Computer Graphics.

My scene contains a 6-walled Cornell Box, each with a different color/pattern. The back wall is visible through the sphere on the left. I added a mirror on the front wall to show us a birds eye perspective of the spheres, and show off the multiple-reflections. Used 3D texture mapping to texture map the smaller sphere and cylinder. Makes use of transparency, refractivity, and reflectivity to simulate glass-like textures on the spheres. Programmed proper shadow definitions for each object.

Advanced features: Anti-aliasing, multiple-reflections, refractive sphere, cylinder objects with caps, and fog.

Alien World - COSC363 project

OpenGL project for COSC363 Computer Graphics.

My scene depicts alien entities exploring a foreign planet with low gravity. While two of the aliens collect samples. One takes off into the atmosphere. For this project I made use of 3D mathematics to set up the camera and objects moving around the scene. The user is able to move the camera throughout the scene, trigger the UFO take-off animation, and reset the scene. I used a skydome and plane to create the environment, and threw in some background "satellites".

Advanced features: planar shadows on all objects, skydome, sweep surfaces and surfaces of revolution, 3D texture mapping.
